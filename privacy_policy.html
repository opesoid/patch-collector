Privacy Policy for Patch Collector

Last Updated: 4-April-2025

Introduction

At Patch Collector, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects the following limited information:

  • Extension Settings: We store your extension preferences (such as enabled/disabled state) locally in Chrome's storage API.
  • Website Data: When you use our extension on the Line6 CustomTone website, we temporarily process patch information that appears on the page to facilitate patch downloads.

We do not collect:

  • Your personal identification information
  • Your browsing history
  • Any information from other websites
  • Any login credentials or passwords

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Enabling patch downloads from Line6 CustomTone
  • Saving your extension preferences
  • Improving the functionality of the extension

Data Storage and Security

All settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers, except when:

  • Communicating with the Line6 CustomTone website to fetch patch information
  • Downloading patches that you explicitly request

Third-Party Access

We do not share any information with third parties. Our extension only interacts with the Line6 CustomTone website for the sole purpose of helping you download patches.

Permissions We Request

Our extension requests the following permissions, and here's why we need them:

  • activeTab: This allows our extension to interact with the Line6 CustomTone website when you're actively using it. We need this to identify and process patch information on the page.
  • storage: This allows our extension to save your preferences, such as whether the extension is enabled or disabled.
  • Host Permission (line6.com): This allows our extension to operate specifically on the Line6 CustomTone website.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable or uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear all stored settings by clearing your browser data

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
