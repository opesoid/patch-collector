User Guide

⚠️ IMPORTANT You MUST be logged into your Line6 CustomTone account to download patches. This extension enhances the download functionality, but it cannot bypass the login requirement.

Introduction

Welcome to the Patch Collector extension! This tool enhances your experience on the CustomTone website by providing advanced features for downloading and organizing guitar patches.

Key features include:

  • Download patches from multiple pages at once
  • Search and filter patches before downloading
  • Track download progress
  • Customize your experience with various settings

Supported Devices

Patch Collector works with Line6 guitar processors including:

  • Helix Floor, LT, Rack, and Native
  • HX Effects and HX Stomp
  • Pod Go and Pod Go Wireless

Getting Started

Prerequisites

  1. Visit Line6 CustomTone
  2. Sign in to your Line6 account (or create one if you don't have it)
  3. Make sure you're logged in before attempting to download any patches

Enabling the Extension

After installing the extension, you'll need to enable it to use its features:

  1. Click on the Patch Collector icon in your browser's extension toolbar
  2. Toggle the switch at the top to enable the extension
  3. When the toggle is green, the extension is active

Once enabled, the extension interface will automatically appear when you visit the CustomTone website.

Interface Overview

The extension adds a panel to the CustomTone page with three main tabs:

  • Settings: Configure download options and preferences
  • Search: Filter and search patches before downloading
  • Bulk: Download multiple pages of patches at once

Settings Tab

The Settings tab allows you to customize your download preferences:

Device Selection

Choose your preferred device from the dropdown menu. This helps filter patches specifically designed for your device.

Sorting Options

You can sort patches by:

  • Popular (most downloaded)
  • Rating (highest rated)

Search Tab

The Search tab lets you find specific patches before downloading:

  1. Enter search terms in the search box
  2. Use filters to narrow results by genre, rating, etc.
  3. Click "Search" to find matching patches
  4. Review the results in the list
  5. Select patches you want to download by checking the boxes
  6. Click "Download Selected" to save only those patches

Downloading Current Page

To download all patches from the current page you're viewing:

  1. Navigate to the page with patches you want
  2. Open the extension panel
  3. Click the "Download Current Page" button in the Search tab

Bulk Tab

The Bulk tab is for downloading multiple pages of patches at once:

  1. Enter the number of pages you want to download (default is 5)
  2. Start from the first page of results
  3. Use search filters if needed
  4. Click "Download All Patches"
Note Bulk downloads can take some time depending on the number of pages and your internet connection speed. The extension will show a progress indicator.

Download Progress

During downloads, a progress bar will appear showing:

  • Number of patches downloaded
  • Total patches to download
  • Current download speed
  • Estimated time remaining

You can cancel a download at any time by clicking the "Cancel" button next to the progress bar.

Tips and Tricks

  • Naming convention: Patches are saved with a consistent naming format: [Artist] - [Patch Name] - [Device].hlx (or appropriate extension)
  • Download location: All patches are saved to your browser's default download location
  • Memory usage: For very large bulk downloads, consider breaking them into smaller batches
  • Filtering: Use genre filters to find patches that match your playing style

Troubleshooting

Extension Not Appearing

If the extension panel doesn't appear on the CustomTone website:

  1. Check that the extension is enabled (the toggle should be green)
  2. Refresh the CustomTone page
  3. Ensure you're on a valid page at line6.com/customtone/

Downloads Not Starting

If patches aren't downloading:

  1. Check that you're logged into your Line6 account
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Make sure your browser's download settings allow multiple downloads
  4. Ensure you haven't blocked downloads from the CustomTone site
  5. Try restarting your browser
